List of commands:

block
	- block a new site
redirect
	- redirect a new site
throttle
	- throttle a new site
del
	- delete a site from the block/redirect/throttle lists
viewblock
	- view list of blocked sites
viewredirect
	- view list of redirected sites
	- also see where sites are redirected to
viewthrottle
	- view list of throttled sites
	- also see the throttle delay
done
	- finish editing the block/redirect/throttle listings
